    Project

    Project

    Beyond RGB looks to provide museums with software that can color calibrate images. By uploading Darkfield, Flatfield, Target, and Artwork images taken under 2 different lighting conditions, Beyond RGB will calibrate the color of the image to be accurate to what the artist intended


    This is a multi-year RIT Software Engineering Project starting in the fall of 2021
